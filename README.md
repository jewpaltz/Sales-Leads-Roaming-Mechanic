# Sales Leads Roaming Mechanic

## Description

This is a text based repository of leads for my business.

### Scope:
- This is not an application, but a repository of leads for my business.
- Gemini pro in Antigravity is used to generate the leads.
- The leads are stored in a markdown file.

### Product Description:
- The product we are selling is a service that companies sign up for to have a mechanic come to their location to perform maintenance on their cars and vans.
- **Service Constraint:** We do NOT service large trucks; only cars and vans.
- **Service Constraint:** We do not do most repairs, only maintenance.

### Target Audience:
- The target audience is companies that have multiple vehicles that need maintenance.
- The taget geographical area is Rockland County, Orange County, and Sulivan County, New York. 

### Lead Format:
- The leads are stored in a markdown file.
- The leads are formatted as follows:
    - Company Name
    - Contact Name
    - Contact Phone Number
    - Contact Email Address
    - Number of Vehicles

### AI Tasks:
- Generate leads for the product.
- Search the internet for companies that have multiple vehicles that need maintenance in the target area.
- Find the person or people that can make the decision to sign up for the product.
- Find the contact information for the person or people that can make the decision to sign up for the product.
- Find pertinant information about the company.
- Find interests of the responsible person and ways to connect with them.
