## Status Legend
*   **To Research**: Need to verify if they have a fleet.
*   **To Call**: Contact identified, ready for first outreach.
*   **Contacted**: Spoke to someone, waiting for decision/meeting.
*   **Customer**: Active client.
*   **Not Interested**: Do not contact for 6 months.
